# Jack Daniel’s–Style Sauce (Copycat)

### Ingredients
* **2 cups** pineapple juice
* **1⅓ cups** brown sugar (packed)
* **½ cup** teriyaki sauce
* **2 tablespoons** soy sauce
* **2 tablespoons** Dijon mustard
* **2 tablespoons** Worcestershire sauce
* **4 teaspoons** lemon juice (or 1 tablespoon + 1 teaspoon)
* **½ cup** Jack Daniel’s whiskey
* **2 tablespoons** finely minced onion
* **2 teaspoons** minced garlic
* **1 teaspoon** black pepper
* **Optional:** ½ teaspoon smoked paprika (for extra depth)

### Instructions
1.  **Combine** all ingredients in a saucepan over medium heat. [cite: 1]
2.  **Bring** to a gentle boil, then reduce heat to low. [cite: 2]
3.  **Simmer** 40–45 minutes, stirring often, until thick and glossy. [cite: 3]

> **Tip:** With a double batch, use a wider saucepan to help it reduce evenly and prevent scorching. [cite: 4]
