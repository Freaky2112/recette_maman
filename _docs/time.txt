RECIPE TIME ESTIMATES & PORTIONS
=================================

Recipe                          | Prep     | Cook / Rest          | Portions
--------------------------------|----------|----------------------|-----------------
Gâteau éclair au chocolat       | 15 min   | 25 min               | 8–10 slices
Gâteau renversé miel & ananas   | 15 min   | 50–55 min            | 8–10 slices
Muffins au All Bran             | 10 min   | 15–20 min            | 12 muffins
Muffins au gruau                | 15 min   | 20 min               | 12 muffins
Pudding au riz crémeux          | 10 min   | 45 min               | 6–8 portions
Poudding chômeur                | 20 min   | 40 min               | 8–10 portions
Taralli aux fenouil             | 30 min   | 4 h rise + ~20 min   | ~60–80 pieces
Tarte aux pêches à la guimauve  | 25 min   | 3–4 h chill          | 8 slices
Tiramisu                        | 30 min   | 2–4 h chill          | 8–10 portions


NOTES
=====

- Taralli aux fenouil is by far the biggest time commitment — with 15 cups of flour,
  this is clearly a large family batch. Budget about 5 hours total (30 min active work
  + 4 hours of rising). The baking time isn't specified in the recipe, so ~20 min at
  420°F is an estimate.

- Tarte aux pêches and Tiramisu are no-bake (or nearly so) but need several hours of
  refrigeration, so plan ahead if serving the same day.

- Both puddings (riz crémeux and chômeur) are straightforward weeknight desserts —
  under an hour start to finish.
