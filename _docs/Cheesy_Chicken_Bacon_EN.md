# Cheesy Chicken Bacon Ranch Tater Tot

### Ingredients
* **4 cups** cooked chicken, shredded or diced [cite: 14]
* **1 can (10.5 oz)** cream of chicken soup [cite: 14]
* **1 cup** sour cream [cite: 14]
* **1 cup** ranch dressing [cite: 14]
* **2 cups** shredded cheddar cheese (divided) [cite: 14]
* **8 slices** cooked bacon, crumbled (reserve some for topping) [cite: 14]
* **1 bag (32 oz)** frozen tater tots [cite: 14]
* **½ tsp** garlic powder [cite: 14]
* **½ tsp** onion powder [cite: 14]
* **½ tsp** black pepper [cite: 14]
* **2 tbsp** fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish) [cite: 14]

### Directions
1.  **Preheat** oven to 190°C (375°F). [cite: 14]
2.  **Grease** a 9×13-inch baking dish. [cite: 15]
3.  **Mix** cream of chicken soup, sour cream, ranch dressing, garlic powder, onion powder, and black pepper in a large bowl. [cite: 15]
4.  **Stir in** cooked chicken, half of the cheddar cheese, and most of the bacon pieces. [cite: 16]
5.  **Spread** chicken mixture evenly in the baking dish and sprinkle remaining cheddar on top. [cite: 17]
6.  **Arrange** tater tots in a single layer over the mixture. [cite: 18]
7.  **Bake** uncovered for 35–40 minutes, until tater tots are golden and crispy. [cite: 19]
8.  **Remove** from oven and sprinkle with reserved bacon and parsley. [cite: 20]

*Serve hot and cheesy!*
